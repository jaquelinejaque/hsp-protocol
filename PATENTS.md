## Patent Claims Overview

The patent application covers, among other things:

1. **Fail-Closed Execution Architecture**
   Systems where AI actions are blocked by default until human approval is cryptographically verified.

2. **Proof of Supervision Consensus**
   Mechanisms for creating immutable, verifiable records of human oversight decisions.

3. **Mathematical Risk Scoring Framework**
   Methods for calculating AI system risk using specific mathematical formulas including sigmoid functions with Feigenbaum constants.

4. **Blockchain-Based Audit Trails**
   Systems for recording AI supervision events on distributed ledgers.

5. **Non-Bypassable Supervision Layers**
   Architectural patterns preventing AI systems from circumventing human oversight.

*Note: This is a simplified summary. The full patent application contains 70 detailed claims.*
